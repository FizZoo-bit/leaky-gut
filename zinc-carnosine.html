Zinc Carnosine: Japan’s Secret Weapon for Gut Healing

Originally developed and studied in Japan, zinc carnosine (polaprezinc) is a complex of zinc and L-carnosine. It has been shown in multiple studies to help repair mucosal lining and reduce inflammation in the gastrointestinal tract.

How It Helps Leaky Gut

  • Promotes regeneration of damaged gut cells
  • Reduces oxidative stress and inflammation
  • Improves mucosal barrier function

Backed by Research

Studies show that zinc carnosine improves outcomes in patients with ulcers and irritable bowel conditions. It also increases gut lining thickness and strength.
