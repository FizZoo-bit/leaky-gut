What Should You Eat to Heal Leaky Gut?

Your gut lining is made of delicate epithelial cells that regenerate every few days—but only if given the right fuel. A leaky gut healing diet focuses on anti-inflammatory, nutrient-dense foods that support cell repair and reduce gut permeability.

🛒 Core Healing Foods

  • 🥩 Bone Broth – rich in gelatin and glutamine to seal gut lining
  • 🍳 Pasture-raised eggs – a great source of lecithin and bioavailable protein
  • 🥬 Leafy Greens – spinach, kale, arugula for folate and fiber
  • 🫐 Berries – antioxidants to combat inflammation
  • 🧄 Fermented foods – kimchi, sauerkraut, kefir for probiotic repopulation

🚫 Foods to Avoid

  • Gluten & wheat-based products
  • Refined sugar and alcohol
  • Industrial seed oils (canola, soybean)
  • Processed snacks and artificial sweeteners
